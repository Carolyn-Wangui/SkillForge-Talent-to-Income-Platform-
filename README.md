## About
SkillForge is a talent-to-income platform I built to break the experience barrier for students and young professionals. My platform connects skilled individuals with real projects, internships, and mentorship, transforming learning into earning while building credibility, independence, and future leaders.

## Mission
To empower young people by connecting their skills to real-world opportunities, enabling them to learn, earn, and grow, while fostering professional credibility and independence.

## Vision
A world where students and young professionals don’t have to wait until graduation to start their careers — they build experience, earn income, and develop confidence while still learning.

## MVP Features
- Simple login & registration
- User skill profiles
- Project listings & applications
